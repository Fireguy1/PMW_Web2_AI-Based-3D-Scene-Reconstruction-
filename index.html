Why We Exist

Memories are more
than records.

In an increasingly digital world, the stories that shape us — the traditions, the voices, the moments — quietly disappear. HeriTeq exists to change that. We combine artificial intelligence with deep human empathy to ensure every story finds a way to live on.

We believe memories are foundations for identity, healing, and belonging. Not just archives — living inheritance.

Story Organisation
